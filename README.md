# Outreach Performance Analysis & Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes outreach operations data to uncover performance patterns, improve data reliability, and support decision-making through clear KPIs and visual dashboards.  
The work focuses on **data cleaning, metric design, and insight communication**, culminating in a structured analytical dashboard suitable for operational and strategic review.

---

- Clean and standardize highly inconsistent outreach data
- Derive reliable country information from phone number prefixes
- Measure outreach effectiveness and connection outcomes
- Identify campaign performance concentration and attempt efficiency
- Build clear, decision-oriented visualizations

## Key Questions Answered

### 1. How effective is the outreach effort overall?
**Answer:**  
A significant portion of outreach attempts result in successful connections, but effectiveness varies by campaign and attempt count.

---

### 2. How many attempts are typically needed to achieve a connection?
**Answer:**  
Most successful responses occur within the first **one to three attempts**. Beyond this point, returns diminish sharply.

---

### 3. Are outreach efforts evenly distributed across campaigns?
**Answer:**  
No. A small number of campaigns account for a disproportionate share of outreach volume, indicating concentration risk and optimization opportunities.

---

### 4. Can unreliable country data be corrected without external APIs?
**Answer:**  
Yes. Phone number prefixes provide a more reliable source of country attribution than free-text entries when properly standardized.

---

### 5. What time patterns exist in outreach volume?
**Answer:**  
Outreach activity shows clear month-to-month variation, highlighting periods of higher engagement and potential resource strain.

---

## Core KPIs Tracked
- **Total Outreach:** Count of valid Reference IDs
- **Total Connected:** Count of successful connection outcomes
- **Connection Distribution:** Connected vs Not Connected
- **Attempt Response Distribution:** Outreach efficiency by attempt count
- **Top Campaigns:** Highest outreach volume contributors

## Key Insights
- Data quality directly impacts insight credibility
- Early outreach attempts deliver the highest value
- Campaign performance is highly uneven
- Standardization rules are more effective than manual fixes
- Clear dashboards outperform dense reports for decision-making

---

## Recommendations
- Focus outreach strategy on early-attempt optimization
- Scale high-performing campaigns; reassess low-impact ones
- Enforce validation rules at data entry
- Use phone-derived country logic as a standard practice
- Treat dashboards as operational tools, not static reports
